03 — Full Funnel Structure
🔴

Top of Funnel (TOF) — Awareness

Cold audiences. Broad or interest-based targeting. Goal: impressions, video views. Reels + Video work best.

50–60% budget
🟡

Middle of Funnel (MOF) — Consideration

Warm audiences (video viewers, page engagers). Goal: website traffic, add to cart. Carousel + Static.

25–30% budget
🟢

Bottom of Funnel (BOF) — Conversion

Hot retargeting: cart abandoners, product viewers, past purchasers. DPA + strong offer copy + urgency.

15–20% budget
Structure: 1 Campaign → 3–5 Ad Sets (audiences) → 3–5 Ads each. Use CBO at campaign level for AI optimization across ad sets.

05 — Content Creation Framework
🎥

UGC Video Ads

Authentic 15–30s creator-style videos. Hook in 3s, problem-solution-CTA structure.

Ratio: 9:16 Reels/Stories, 4:5 Feed
Length: 15–30 seconds optimal
Captions always on (85% watch muted)
🖼️

Static Image Ads

High-contrast product images with bold headline overlays. Single clear value prop.

Size: 1080×1080 or 1080×1350
Text: under 20% of image area
Test: 3–5 variants per offer
📱

Story / Reel Ads

Full-screen vertical. Pattern interrupt in first frame is critical to stopping scroll.

Format: 1080×1920 (9:16)
Keep content in middle 80%
Motion increases CTR 30%+
📦

Carousel Ads

First card = scroll-stopping hook. Each card = one benefit. Last card = CTA.

Cards: 3–7 (sweet spot: 4–5)
Consistent visual theme
Use for: multi-product, before/after
